myTunes

Your music,movies and TV shows take centre stage.

myTunes is the best way to organize and enjoy the music, movies, and TV shows you already have - and shop for the ones.Enjoy all the entartainment myTunes has to offer on your Mac and PC.

Music

Stream over 45 million songs, ad-free. Or download albums and tracks to listen to offline. All the music in your personal myTunes library-no matter where it came from - lives right alongside the Orange Music catelog. Start your free three-month trial with no commitment, and cancel anytime.

Orange Music is available in myTunes, and for iOS and Android devices.

The movie and TV collection you always wished for Granted.

With over 100,000 movies and TV shows to choose from, there's always something great to watch on myTunes and if you watch on Orange TV 4K, you'll be able to enjoy a tremendous selection of your favorite content in 4K HDR. So get ready to enjoy episodes of your favorite TV shows or hit movies you've been waiting to see-anytime,anywhere. Just tap to play, or even download if you're going somewhere you won't have WI-FI.
